Recipe Details

Cheesecake

Description

A rich and creamy dessert made with a buttery biscuit crust and smooth cream cheese filling.

Ingredients

  • 200g digestive biscuits
  • 100g butter
  • 300g cream cheese
  • 100g sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Crush the biscuits and mix with melted butter to form the crust.
  2. Press the crust into a pan and chill.
  3. Mix cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Pour the mixture over the crust and chill until set.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
